## 💡 Pricing Psychology

### Why $14.99 Works Better Than $8

**$8/month Problems:**
- ❌ Signals "cheap" or "basic"
- ❌ Users question quality
- ❌ Hard to cover AI costs
- ❌ Difficult to raise later
- ❌ Attracts price-sensitive users (high churn)

**$14.99/month Benefits:**
- ✅ Premium positioning
- ✅ Attracts committed users
- ✅ Better retention (invested users)
- ✅ Room for costs and profit
- ✅ Can offer discounts from higher base

### The "Goldilocks" Principle

```
Too Cheap:  $4.99-7.99  → "Is this even good?"
Just Right: $12.99-19.99 → "This is valuable"
Too High:   $29.99+      → "Too expensive for me"
```

Your sweet spot: **$14.99**
