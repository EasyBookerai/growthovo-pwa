# Growthovo Gamification User Guide

Welcome to the Growthovo gamification system! This guide explains how to make the most of our premium features, earn XP, and level up.

## ⚡ XP and Leveling Up
XP (Experience Points) is the primary way to track your progress in Growthovo.

- **Lesson Completion**: +20 XP
- **Daily Check-in**: +5 XP
- **Streak Milestones**: 50–500 XP bonus
- **Level Up**: +100 XP bonus

As you earn XP, you'll level up. Each level requires more XP than the last, challenging you to stay consistent.

## 🔥 Streaks
Consistency is key to personal growth.
- **Daily Streak**: Keep your streak alive by completing at least one lesson or check-in every day.
- **Streak Freezes**: Use a freeze to protect your streak if you miss a day. You can hold up to 3 freezes.
- **Milestones**: Reach 7, 30, 100, and 365 days for special badges and XP rewards.

## 🏆 Achievements
Unlock badges for various accomplishments!
- **Streak Badges**: For hitting consistency milestones.
- **Lesson Badges**: For completing quantities of lessons.
- **Social Badges**: For inviting friends or joining squads.
- **Special Badges**: For first lessons, perfect weeks, and more.

## 🎯 Daily Goals
Each day, you'll be given 3 goals to complete.
- **Easy**: Simple tasks to keep you moving.
- **Medium**: Moderate challenges for your core pillars.
- **Hard**: Stretch goals for maximum XP rewards.

## 📈 Leaderboard
Compete with others in your league!
- **Promotion Zone**: Finish in the top 5 to move up to a higher league tier.
- **Relegation Zone**: Avoid the bottom 5 to stay in your current tier.
- **Weekly Reset**: Leaderboards reset every Sunday at midnight.

## 🎊 Celebrations
Enjoy the confetti! You'll see celebration modals for major milestones like level-ups, streak milestones, and achievement unlocks. These summarize your progress and rewards.

## 📱 Performance & Accessibility
- **Glassmorphism**: Experience our premium frosted glass UI. If your device is struggling, the app will automatically simplify effects to maintain 60fps.
- **Accessibility**: All features are fully compatible with screen readers (VoiceOver/TalkBack).
