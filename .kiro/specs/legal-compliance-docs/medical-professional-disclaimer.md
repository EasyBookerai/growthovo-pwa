## Understanding Rex: Our AI Coach

Rex is an artificial intelligence system powered by advanced language models. While Rex is designed to be helpful, supportive, and personalized, it is important to understand the limitations of AI technology:

### AI Capabilities

Rex can:
- Remember your past conversations and progress within Growthovo
- Provide personalized motivational messages and encouragement
- Suggest strategies for habit formation and self-improvement
- Help you reflect on your goals and challenges
- Offer general guidance based on established self-improvement principles

### AI Limitations

Rex cannot:
- **Understand complex human emotions** with the depth and nuance of a trained human professional
- **Diagnose medical or mental health conditions** or provide clinical assessments
- **Provide emergency assistance** or crisis intervention
- **Replace human judgment** in important life decisions
- **Guarantee accuracy** in all responses - AI systems can make mistakes, provide incomplete information, or generate inappropriate responses
- **Understand your full context** beyond what you share in the app
- **Provide professional expertise** in specialized fields like medicine, psychology, or finance

### Potential for Inaccuracies

AI-generated content may contain:
- Factual errors or outdated information
- Responses that don't fully understand your unique situation
- Suggestions that may not be appropriate for your specific circumstances
- Misinterpretations of your messages or intent

**Always use your own judgment when considering AI-generated advice. If something doesn't feel right or seems inappropriate, trust your instincts and seek human professional guidance.**

You should consult qualified professionals in the following situations:

### Medical Concerns
- Physical symptoms or health problems
- Chronic pain or illness
- Medication questions
- Diagnosis or treatment of any medical condition
- Preventive health care

**Contact:** Your primary care physician, specialist, or healthcare provider

### Mental Health Concerns
- Persistent feelings of sadness, anxiety, or hopelessness
- Difficulty functioning in daily life
- Trauma or abuse

- Thoughts of self-harm or suicide

**Contact:** Licensed psychologist, psychiatrist, therapist, or counselor

### Financial Concerns

- Retirement planning

- Major financial decisions

## Emergency Situations

### If You Are in Crisis

**Growthovo is NOT an emergency service and cannot provide crisis intervention.**

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, mental health crisis, or thoughts of self-harm or suicide, please:

**Immediate Emergency:**
- **Call emergency services:** 112 (EU) / 911 (US)
- Go to your nearest emergency room
- Call your local emergency number

**Mental Health Crisis:**
- **Romania:** Telefonul Sufletului: 0800 801 200
- **EU Suicide Prevention:** 116 123
- **International Association for Suicide Prevention:** https://www.iasp.info/resources/Crisis_Centres/

**Crisis Text Lines:**
- Many countries offer crisis support via text message
- Search for "crisis text line [your country]" for local resources

**Do not wait. Do not rely on Growthovo in emergency situations. Seek immediate professional help.**
