## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Where We Store Your Data
Your data is primarily stored in the European Union through our hosting providers. Some data may be processed in the United States by our AI service providers.

### 5.2 How We Protect Your Data
- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit (using TLS) and at rest
- **Access Controls:** Only authorized personnel can access user data, and only when necessary
- **Regular Security Audits:** We regularly review our security practices
- **Secure Authentication:** We use industry-standard authentication methods
- **Monitoring:** We monitor for suspicious activity and security threats

### 5.3 Data Retention Periods
We keep your data for as long as necessary to provide our service and comply with legal obligations:

- **Account Information:** Until you delete your account, plus 30 days for backup purposes
- **Chat History with Rex:** Until you delete your account or request deletion
- **Progress Data:** Until you delete your account
- **Payment Records:** 7 years (required for tax and accounting purposes)
- **Analytics Data:** Up to 2 years in anonymized form
- **Support Communications:** 3 years

After these periods, we securely delete or anonymize your data.

## 6. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a user in the European Union, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### 6.1 Right to Access
You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

**How to Exercise:** Go to Settings > Data Management > Export My Data, or email privacy@growthovo.app

### 6.2 Right to Rectification
You can correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

**How to Exercise:** Update your information in Settings > Profile, or contact us at privacy@growthovo.app

### 6.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to Be Forgotten")
You can request that we delete your personal data.

**How to Exercise:** Go to Settings > Data Management > Delete My Account, or email privacy@growthovo.app

**Note:** Some data may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., payment records for tax purposes)

### 6.4 Right to Data Portability
You can receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format and transfer it to another service.

**How to Exercise:** Use the Export My Data feature in Settings, which provides your data in JSON format

### 6.5 Right to Object
You can object to processing of your personal data for certain purposes, such as marketing.

**How to Exercise:** Adjust your preferences in Settings > Privacy Preferences, or email privacy@growthovo.app

### 6.6 Right to Restrict Processing
You can request that we limit how we use your personal data in certain circumstances.

**How to Exercise:** Contact us at privacy@growthovo.app with your specific request

### 6.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
Where we process data based on your consent, you can withdraw that consent at any time.

**How to Exercise:** Adjust your preferences in Settings > Privacy Preferences

### 6.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint
If you believe we've mishandled your personal data, you can file a complaint with your local data protection authority.

**For Romania:** Autoritatea Națională de Supraveghere a Prelucrării Datelor cu Caracter Personal (ANSPDCP)  
Website: www.dataprotection.ro  
Email: anspdcp@dataprotection.ro

**Response Time:** We will respond to your requests within 30 days as required by GDPR.
