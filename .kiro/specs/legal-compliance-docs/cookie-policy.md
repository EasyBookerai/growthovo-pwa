## What Are Cookies and Tracking Technologies?

### Cookies
Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us recognize you and remember your preferences. In mobile applications, similar technologies like local storage and device identifiers serve the same purpose.

### Tracking Technologies
We use various technologies to collect and store information, including:
- **Local Storage:** Data stored directly on your device
- **Session Storage:** Temporary data that expires when you close the app
- **Device Identifiers:** Unique identifiers for your mobile device
- **Analytics SDKs:** Software development kits that collect usage data
- **Authentication Tokens:** Secure tokens that keep you logged in

## Cookies and Technologies We Use

### Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These technologies are necessary for the app to function properly. You cannot opt out of essential cookies as they are required for core functionality.

#### Authentication and Security
- **Purpose:** Keep you securely logged in and protect your account
- **Type:** Authentication tokens, session identifiers
- **Duration:** Until you log out or token expires (typically 30 days)
- **Data Collected:** User ID, session token, login timestamp

- **Type:** Local storage
- **Duration:** Persistent until you clear app data
- **Data Collected:** Language preference, notification settings, theme choice
- **Why Essential:** Ensures the app works according to your preferences

- **Purpose:** Enable core features like Rex conversations and progress tracking
- **Type:** Local storage, session data
- **Duration:** Varies by feature (session to persistent)
- **Data Collected:** Cached data, temporary state information
- **Why Essential:** Required for the app to function smoothly and efficiently

### Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These technologies help us understand how you use Growthovo so we can improve the experience. You can opt out of analytics cookies.

#### Usage Analytics
- **Purpose:** Understand how users interact with features
- **Type:** Analytics SDK (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel)
- **Duration:** Varies (typically 2 years)
- **Data Collected:** 
  - Screen views and navigation patterns

#### Performance Monitoring
- **Purpose:** Monitor app performance and identify technical issues
- **Type:** Performance monitoring SDK
- **Duration:** Varies (typically 90 days)
- **Data Collected:**
  - App load times
  - API response times
  - Crash reports
  - Error logs

- **Third Parties:** Firebase Performance, Sentry (or similar)
- **Why We Use It:** To ensure the app runs smoothly and fix technical problems quickly

### Marketing and Attribution Cookies (Optional)

These technologies help us understand how users discover Growthovo and measure the effectiveness of our marketing efforts.

#### Attribution Tracking
- **Purpose:** Understand which marketing channels bring users to Growthovo
- **Type:** Attribution SDK
- **Duration:** Varies (typically 1 year)
- **Data Collected:**
  - Install source (App Store, Google Play, referral link)

- **Third Parties:** AppsFlyer, Adjust (or similar)
- **Why We Use It:** To measure marketing effectiveness and optimize user acquisition

- **Type:** Device push token

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### In-App Cookie Settings

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time:

1. Open the Growthovo app
2. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy**
3. Tap **Cookie Preferences**
4. Toggle options for:
   - **Analytics Cookies:** ON/OFF
   - **Marketing Cookies:** ON/OFF
   - **Push Notifications:** ON/OFF

**Note:** Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the app to function.

### Device-Level Settings

#### iOS
- **Limit Ad Tracking:** Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
- **Reset Advertising ID:** Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Reset Advertising Identifier
- **Notifications:** Settings > Notifications > Growthovo

#### Android
- **Opt Out of Ads Personalization:** Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **Reset Advertising ID:** Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID
- **Notifications:** Settings > Apps > Growthovo > Notifications

### Clear App Data

To remove all locally stored data:

#### iOS
- Delete and reinstall the app, or
- Settings > General > iPhone Storage > Growthovo > Delete App

#### Android
- Settings > Apps > Growthovo > Storage > Clear Data

**Warning:** Clearing app data will log you out and remove all local preferences. Your account data stored on our servers will remain intact.

## Cookies and Your Privacy

### Data Protection
All data collected through cookies is handled in accordance with:
- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation)
- **ePrivacy Directive** (EU Cookie Law)
- Our [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy.md)

### Your Rights
Under GDPR, you have the right to:
- **Access:** Request information about cookies and data collected
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of data collected through cookies
- **Objection:** Opt out of non-essential cookies
- **Portability:** Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@growthovo.app.

### Data Retention
- **Essential cookies:** Retained as long as necessary for app functionality
- **Analytics cookies:** Typically retained for 2 years, then anonymized or deleted
- **Marketing cookies:** Typically retained for 1 year, then deleted
- **Session data:** Deleted when you log out or session expires

### International Data Transfers
Some third-party services we use may transfer data outside the EU. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
- Privacy Shield certification (where applicable)
- GDPR-compliant data processing agreements
