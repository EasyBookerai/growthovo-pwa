# 🚀 Growthovo - Your Personal Growth Platform

**Growthovo** is a gamified personal development platform that helps you build better habits and master life skills across 6 core growth pillars.

---

## 🎯 What is Growthovo?

Growthovo combines the addictive engagement of Duolingo with comprehensive personal development education. It's your daily growth companion that makes self-improvement feel like a game, not a chore.

### The Core Concept

Instead of overwhelming you with generic advice, Growthovo breaks down personal growth into:
- **6 Life Pillars**: Mental Health, Relationships, Career, Fitness, Finance, Hobbies
- **Bite-sized lessons**: 5-10 minute lessons with practical exercises
- **Daily missions**: Small, achievable actions that build momentum
- **XP & Levels**: Progress tracking that shows your growth journey
- **AI Coach (Rex)**: Personalized guidance and accountability

### 🎮 Gamification System
- **XP Points**: Earn 50 XP per lesson, 30 XP per daily challenge
- **Levels**: Progress through levels as you complete lessons
- **Streaks**: Daily check-ins keep you accountable
- **Leagues**: Compete with others in weekly leaderboards (Bronze → Diamond)
- **Achievements**: Unlock badges for milestones

### 📚 Interactive Lessons
- **Multiple Choice**: Test your knowledge
- **True/False**: Challenge assumptions
- **Fill-in-the-Blank**: Reinforce key concepts
- **Scenarios**: Apply learning to real situations
- **Reflection**: Deep personal insights

- **Personalized Advice**: Tailored to your goals and progress
- **Accountability**: Check-ins and reminders

- Resets weekly for fresh competition

### 📊 Progress Tracking
- **Dashboard**: See your growth at a glance
- **Pillar Progress**: Track completion across all 6 areas
- **Streak Counter**: Visualize your consistency
- **XP History**: Review your learning journey

## 🎯 How It Works

### Daily Flow
1. **Check In** (2 min)
   - Set your daily intention
   - Review yesterday's wins
   - Get your personalized mission

2. **Complete a Lesson** (5-10 min)
   - Choose a pillar
   - Work through interactive exercises
   - Earn 50 XP + insights

3. **Daily Challenge** (5-15 min)
   - Small actionable task
   - Earn 30 XP
   - Build real-world habits

4. **Chat with Rex** (optional)
   - Get personalized guidance
   - Troubleshoot challenges
   - Celebrate wins

5. **Check League Standing**
   - See your weekly rank
   - Compete with peers
   - Climb the leaderboard

### Weekly Cycle
- **Monday**: League resets, new competition begins
- **Daily**: Complete lessons, challenges, check-ins
- **Sunday**: Final push for league promotion
- **Repeat**: Continuous growth and competition
